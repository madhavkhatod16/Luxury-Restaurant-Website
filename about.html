About Royal Feast

Our Beginning

A Legacy Born
in Passion

Royal Feast was founded in 2008 by Chef Arjun Mehta with a singular vision: to create a dining destination in Sambhajinagar that rivalled the finest restaurants in the world — not through imitation, but through authenticity.

What began as an intimate 30-seat bistro on Palace Road has grown into a landmark of Indian fine dining, celebrated for its marriage of classical French technique with the bold, complex spice palette of Maharashtra.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries with it a story of tradition, innovation, and an unyielding respect for the finest ingredients.
